ABOUT RAIN

Rain V. Britannia is the son of Emperor Lelouch Vi Britannia and the 69th Successor of the Throne. Prince Rain was born on June 8, 2008. After he was taken out of her mother's womb, his father immediately took him to the Laboratory Experiment called "AEVERSION2" (also known as the Albert Einstein Version 2), an experiment where the scientists tried their best to resurrect the soul of Albert Einstein. After many attempts, all of them failed. The last participant in the laboratory was none other than Prince Rain. After successfully putting Albert Einstein's soul into his body, he woke and said the word "E=mc2." Prince Rain was renamed Rain G. Delmo in order for the world not to find out about the return of Albert Einstein. His father, Lelouch Vi Brittania, sent him to a place called San Jose Del Monte, Bulacan, and the rest is history.
